My Story

My journey into technology began with a bold challenge: building my first gaming computer from scratch with zero prior experience. That hands-on dive into hardware, operating systems, and troubleshooting ignited a passion that has driven my career ever since.

With a background in Criminal Justice, I've developed a unique ability to think outside the box when solving complex problems. This analytical mindset, combined with my experience in community service and coaching, has shaped me into a collaborative team player who thrives in dynamic environments.
